Welcome to M-DR Laboratory

The M-DR Lab focuses on the dynamics, durability, and reliability of marine mechanical systems, including advanced propulsion, intelligent O&M, friction-lubrication technology, and safety-critical transport equipment.

Research Areas

  • Ship propulsion and mechanical transmission systems
  • Intelligent operation and maintenance (O&M) of marine equipment
  • Friction, lubrication, and reliability of mechanical systems
  • Smart technologies for dredging, deep sea mining, and ship lifts
  • Oil and gas storage, transportation, and safety risk assessment

Research Subjects

  • Sensor Monitoring Systems
  • Dredging Vessels and Equipment
  • Pipeline Transportation Systems
  • Marine Shafting Systems
  • Gearboxes
  • Centrifugal Pumps
  • LNG/LPG Cargo Tanks
