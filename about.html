About M-DR Laboratory

Affiliated with Wuhan University of Technology, the M-DR Lab focuses on both fundamental and applied research in marine mechanical systems. Our mission is to enhance the dynamics, durability, and reliability of ship propulsion systems and marine equipment.

Through theoretical modeling, experimental validation, and intelligent analytics, our team advances the sustainable development and safe operation of marine machinery.

The lab collaborates globally with academia and industry, striving for breakthroughs in digital maintenance, fault diagnostics, and system resilience.
